ABOUT US

OVERVIEW

DoBu Martial Arts is a premier martial arts gym that prides itself on providing top-notch training and services to its members. Our gym offers a wide range of classes in various martial arts disciplines, including Judo, Jiu-Jitsu, Karate, and Muay Thai, taught by experienced and accredited coaches. Whether you're a seasoned martial artist or a beginner, our classes are tailored to meet your needs and help you achieve your fitness goals.

At DoBu Martial Arts, we also offer fitness training and self-defense courses, so you can train in the martial arts discipline of your choice while also improving your overall fitness and self-defense skills. Our fully-equipped gym features state-of-the-art equipment and facilities, including a large, matted martial arts area, a sauna, a steam room, and changing and shower facilities.

Our gym is not just a place to work out, it's a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for martial arts and fitness. We welcome members of all ages and abilities, and our friendly and supportive environment ensures that you'll feel right at home. So whether you're looking to compete at a high level or simply looking to get in shape and learn some new skills, DoBu Martial Arts is the gym for you. Join us today and start your journey towards a healthier, happier you!

FACILITIES

MARTIAL ARTS ROOM

The martial arts area at DoBu Martial Arts is a spacious and well-equipped facility dedicated to various martial arts disciplines. It features a large matted floor that provides a safe and comfortable training space for students. Whether you're interested in karate, judo, jiu-jitsu, or other martial arts styles, this area offers the perfect environment for learning and practicing.

SAUNA ROOM

Indulge in the rejuvenating benefits of our steam room. The steam-filled enclosure creates a moist and relaxing environment that helps open up pores, cleanse the skin, and promote detoxification. Enjoy the enveloping warmth as the steam works its magic, leaving you feeling refreshed and invigorated.

STEAM ROOM

Relax and rejuvenate in our sauna, a haven of warmth and tranquility. The sauna offers a dry heat environment that helps soothe muscles, relieve stress, and promote overall well-being. Step inside and let the gentle heat envelop you, providing a perfect way to unwind after a vigorous workout or martial arts session.

CHANGING ROOM

At DoBu Martial Arts, we provide dedicated changing facilities to meet your needs. Our changing rooms are spacious and well-maintained, offering a private and comfortable space for you to prepare for your training session. Equipped with lockers, benches, and ample storage, our changing facilities allow you to securely store your belongings while you focus on your workout. Whether you're putting on your martial arts gear or changing into your workout attire, our changing facilities offer convenience and privacy.

SHOWER ROOM

After an intense training session, our shower facilities are designed to help you freshen up and rejuvenate. We understand the importance of hygiene and providing a clean environment for our members. Our shower areas feature modern fixtures and amenities, ensuring a refreshing experience. Step into our well-maintained shower cubicles, let the warm water wash away the sweat and grime, and leave feeling invigorated. Our commitment to cleanliness extends to our shower facilities, ensuring a pleasant and hygienic experience for all.

GYM FACILITIES

Our fully-equipped gym is designed to cater to all your fitness needs. It houses a wide range of exercise equipment, including cardio machines, weightlifting stations, and strength training tools. Whether you're looking to build muscle, improve endurance, or enhance your overall fitness level, our gym provides a diverse array of options to help you achieve your goals.
